## A Note on this Fork
BloomRPC has been an incredible tool for the gRPC community, standing out for its simplicity and efficiency when other alternatives felt bloated or overly complex. I personally rely on this software for my daily workflow, as I have yet to find another tool that matches its speed and practical approach to exploring gRPC services.

While the original project was archived in early 2023, I decided to take it upon myself to maintain it and keep it running on modern systems. 

**Huge thanks to the original creators and contributors at UtilityWarehouse** for building such a fantastic piece of software. Your work has been invaluable, and I am honored to help keep the spirit of this project alive.

## What's New in this Version (1.6.0)
This version focuses on modernization and improving the developer experience with a few quality-of-life additions:

*   **Modernized Stack:** Updated Electron to **v27.3.0** and refreshed key dependencies (Node.js >=18, React 18, Ant Design 5, etc.) to ensure stability and compatibility with modern operating systems.
*   **Resizable Sidebar:** Added the long-awaited ability to resize the sidebar, allowing for better management of your service list.
*   **"Close All" Feature:** Added a quick action to close all open tabs/requests, making it easier to clean up your workspace in one click.
